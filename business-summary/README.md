# Global Sales & Logistics Summary Dashboard

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of **global sales performance, logistics volume, and individual sales contributions** across multiple international markets.

The objective is to evaluate regional performance, shipment activity, and top sales representatives to better understand revenue distribution and operational efficiency.

---

## Key Performance Indicators

| Metric | Value |
|------|------|
| Total Revenue | $6,183,625 |
| Boxes Shipped | 177,007 |
| Total Shipments | 1,094 |

---

## Core Visualizations

### Sales by Country

A comparative bar chart showing revenue distribution across six global markets.

Key observations:

- **Australia** generates the highest revenue.
- **UK and India** closely follow as strong contributors.
- **USA, Canada, and New Zealand** show steady but slightly lower sales volumes.

---

### Top 10 Sales Representatives

A ranking of the highest-performing sales personnel based on revenue generated.

- **Top Performer:** Dennison Crosswaite (~$70K revenue)
- **Strong Contributors:** Kelci Walkden and Brien Boise
- Revenue among the top ten ranges from **~$33K to ~$70K**, showing variation in individual performance.

---

## Key Business Insights

| Insight | Interpretation |
|--------|--------|
| Strong Global Distribution | Sales are spread relatively evenly across major markets |
| Top Market | Australia contributes the highest revenue |
| Performance Gap | Revenue difference between top and bottom sales performers exceeds 2× |
