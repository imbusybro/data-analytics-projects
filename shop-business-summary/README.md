# Shop Business Summary Dashboard

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of retail performance, shipping trends, and regional sales distribution across the United States.

The goal of the analysis is to identify **high-performing product categories, shipping patterns, and geographic sales concentration** to support operational and strategic decision-making.

---

## Key Visualizations

### Orders by State
A geographic heat map highlighting order distribution across the United States.  
The map shows strong order concentration in the **Upper Midwest and coastal regions**, with Minnesota recording **89 total orders**.

### Profit by Product Category
A bubble chart comparing profitability across product categories.  
**Office Supplies** emerges as the most profitable category, generating **7,780 in profit**, indicating strong demand and stable margins.

### Shipping Mode Trends
A seasonal bar chart analyzing order volume by shipping mode.

Key observations:
- **Standard Class** is the dominant shipping method.
- Shipping activity peaks in **November**, likely due to holiday demand.
- **February** shows the lowest shipping activity.

### Discount Usage by Order
A detailed table highlighting the number of discounts applied per order.

Some orders (e.g., **US-2022-121783**) show up to **five discounts**, which may impact profit margins and pricing strategy.

## Key Business Insights

| Insight | Interpretation |
|--------|--------|
