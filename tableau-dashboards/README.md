# IMDb Movie Insights Dashboard

This Tableau dashboard explores trends in the **IMDb movie dataset**, analyzing how films have evolved over time in terms of popularity, ratings, and financial performance. The dashboard combines multiple visualizations to highlight patterns in movie production, audience engagement, and genre distribution from the 1920s to the early 2020s.

---

## Dashboard Features

### Top Movies by Popularity
A horizontal bar chart highlighting the **top 10 most voted movies** on IMDb.  
*The Shawshank Redemption* and *The Dark Knight* lead with over **2.3M votes**, indicating strong audience engagement.

### Industry Trends Over Time
- **Gross Revenue by Year:** Shows significant box office growth, particularly after the 1990s.
- **Movies Released per Year:** Displays the steady rise in global film production, peaking in the late 2010s.

### Rating & Critic Analysis
- **Meta Score by Top Genres:** Box plots comparing critic scores across major genres.
- **IMDb Ratings by Top Years:** Statistical breakdown showing how audience ratings have remained relatively consistent over time.

### Genre & Certification Insights
- **Genre Word Cloud:** Highlights dominant genres, with **Drama** and **Comedy** appearing most frequently.
- **Film Certification Distribution:** Shows that **U (Universal)** and **A (Adult)** are the most common classifications in the dataset.

### Featured Film Metadata
The dashboard includes a **dynamic spotlight panel** that displays key metadata for selected films such as release year, runtime, and meta score.

## Key Insights

- **Industry Expansion:** Both movie releases and total box office revenue have increased significantly since the mid-1990s.
- **Stable Audience Ratings:** Despite the increase in film production, average IMDb ratings remain relatively stable between **7.5–8.5** for top-performing films.
