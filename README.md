# Data Analytics Dashboard Portfolio

This repository contains a collection of **data analytics dashboards** built using Tableau and Excel.  
Each project focuses on analyzing real-world datasets to uncover trends, business insights, and performance metrics.

---

## Projects

### IMDb Movie Insights Dashboard
Analyzes historical movie data to explore trends in film production, ratings, genres, and box office performance.

**Key Insights**
- Growth of movie production over time
- Rating distributions across years
- Popular genres and audience engagement

---

### HR Analytics Dashboard
Examines employee attrition and workforce demographics to identify high-risk departments and retention patterns.

**Key Insights**
- Attrition trends by department and gender
- Workforce age distribution
- Job satisfaction patterns across roles

---

### Shop Business Summary Dashboard
Provides a high-level view of retail performance, shipping activity, and product profitability across the United States.

**Key Insights**
- Profit distribution across product categories
- Shipping mode trends and seasonality
- Regional order concentration

---

### Global Sales & Logistics Dashboard
Analyzes international sales performance and shipment volumes across multiple markets.

**Key Insights**
- Revenue distribution by country
- Top-performing sales representatives
- Logistics volume and shipment efficiency
